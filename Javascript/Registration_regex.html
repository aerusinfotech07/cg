📘 REGEX EXPLANATION (IMPORTANT)

EMAIL REGEX USED:

^[^\s@]+@[^\s@]+\.[^\s@]{2,}$

[^\s@]+  → Any character EXCEPT space and '@'
@         → Exactly one '@' symbol
\.        → Dot character
{2,}      → Minimum 2 characters (com, in, org)

WHY '@' IS EXCLUDED INSIDE [ ] ?
To ensure '@' appears ONLY ONCE and only in the middle.
